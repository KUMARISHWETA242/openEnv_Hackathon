# Motivation

## Why Satellite Constellation Management?

Satellite constellations play a crucial role in modern communication, Earth observation, and navigation systems. Managing these complex systems requires sophisticated decision-making under uncertainty.

## Reinforcement Learning Challenges

This environment addresses key challenges in applying reinforcement learning to real-world satellite operations:

- **Multi-agent coordination**: Managing multiple satellites with interdependent actions
- **Resource constraints**: Balancing power, storage, and communication limitations
- **Dynamic environments**: Adapting to changing weather, task priorities, and orbital conditions
- **Long-term planning**: Optimizing constellation performance over extended time horizons

## OpenEnv Integration

By implementing the OpenEnv specification, this environment enables:
- Standardized benchmarking across different RL algorithms
- Reproducible research with consistent evaluation metrics
- Community-driven development and improvement
- Easy integration with existing RL frameworks
